Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Creator: Arash Alborz

Welcome to the digital edition of Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley’s novel, Frankenstein. This project serves as a window into one of the most influential literary works of all time, carefully crafted as part of the “Text as Data II” course at the University of Antwerp. This website offers a glimpse into the intricate process of Shelley’s storytelling by presenting a digital reconstruction of her original manuscript. By exploring these pages, you’ll not only encounter the novel’s timeless themes of creation, ambition, and humanity but also appreciate the collaborative effort between Mary and Percy Shelley in bringing this masterpiece to life.

Through this project, I aim to merge traditional literature with modern technology, providing a unique opportunity to delve into the manuscript while exploring its historical and textual significance. Enjoy your journey through the pages of Frankenstein; Click on the pages and look around!
